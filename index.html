Welcome to STP Archery

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005. Our state-of-the-art educational and training facility incorporates 28 shooting lanes and 2 private coaching rooms, detailed technical video analysis, and generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors to provide a truly superior experience.

From Beginner to Elite, archers of all ages and ability learn about this exciting Olympic and international sport. We offer group lessons, private classes, birthdays or corporate parties, and competitive tournaments. We are dedicated to teaching valuable life skills to the youth in our community through the wonderful discipline of archery.

Straight to the Point Archery’s staff has decades of experience in the archery industry and affiliations with many national and state archery associations, including the USAA, WSAA, and NFAA. We are committed to educating you with any questions that you may have. Sign up today!
